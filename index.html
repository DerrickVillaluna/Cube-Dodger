Short Description

Cube Dodger is a fast-paced 2D game where players control a cube to dodge obstacles. With simple controls, players navigate through challenging levels, aiming to survive as long as possible. It's a quick and thrilling test of reflexes and skill!.

Features

  • Easy-to-learn controls for players of all skill levels.
  • est your reflexes with increasing speed and difficulty.
  • Compete with yourself or others to achieve the highest score.
  • Runs smoothly on most devices without performance issues.
